Membership FAQs

Your questions answered about LiftVerse Gym memberships

1. What are the membership options available?

We offer various membership plans including monthly, quarterly, and annual memberships. Each plan comes with different benefits and access to our facilities.

2. How can I sign up for a membership?

You can sign up for a membership online by visiting our Membership page or by visiting our gym in person.

3. Can I freeze or cancel my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to three months or cancel it at any time by contacting our support team at support@liftverse.com.

4. Do you offer discounts for students or corporate groups?

Yes, we offer special discounts for students and corporate groups. Please contact us for more details on eligibility and the discount rates.

5. Are there any joining fees?

We do not charge any joining fees. Our memberships are designed to be simple and affordable with no hidden costs.

6. What facilities do I have access to with my membership?

All members have access to our full range of gym equipment, fitness classes, swimming pool, and sauna. Depending on your membership plan, you may also have access to personal training and premium classes.

7. Can I transfer my membership to someone else?

No, memberships are non-transferable. However, if you're unable to use your membership, we can discuss other options with you.

8. Is there a refund policy for membership fees?

Refunds are generally not offered. However, in exceptional cases such as medical reasons, you may contact our support team for assistance.

9. Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your membership at any time. Simply contact our front desk or email support@liftverse.com for assistance.

10. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email at support@liftverse.com or by visiting the contact section of our website.
